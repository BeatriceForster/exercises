# Tabata 3x3x3 Workout

## Instructions
- Perform **3 exercises per round**.
- Each exercise lasts **45 seconds**, followed by a **10-second rest**.
- Repeat each round **3 times** before moving to the next.
- Take a **1-minute rest** between rounds.
